Who we are!

The ethos of the project can be summarized in these three key values:


  • Alleviate some of the struggles of Toronto’s homeless by connecting them with great public services and resources

  • Leverage the power and resources of hackerspaces throughout Toronto for the benefit of the city’s vulnerable population!

The project is run by MisterChalmers, Zachary Donsky, and Joshua Loong.
