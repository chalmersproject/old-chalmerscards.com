Chalmers Cards is a guerilla project bringing information resources to people living in unstable housing conditions. Each set of cards is a laser-cut wooden handout that explains how to access a variety of legal, shelter, food and clothing services.

Current estimates put Toronto's population experiencing homelessness at more than 5'600. More than 500 of these folks are sleeping rough on the street. Many of which are unaware of the different resources that are available to them. Our mission is to gather information and iterate our design to help people in need access everything that is available to them.
