Chalmers Cards is a guerilla project bringing information resources to people living in unstable housing conditions. Each set of cards is a laser-cut wooden handout that explains how to access a variety of legal, shelter, food and clothing services.

There are an estimated 5,600+ homeless people currently living in Toronto by city estimates. Many which are unaware of the different resources that are available to them. Our mission is to gather resources and iterate our design to help people in need access everything that is available to them.
