Chalmers Cards is a guerilla project bringing information resources to people living in unstable housing conditions.

MisterChalmers distributes laser-cut wooden handouts that explain how to access public resources! Stuff like toll free numbers for free legal aid and how to apply for financial aid!

Chalmers Cards are laser-cut onto wood so the information doesn't get crumpled. Plus people think they're cool so the cards don't get lost!

Current estimates put Toronto's population experiencing homelessness at more than 5'600. More than 500 of these folks are sleeping rough on the street.
